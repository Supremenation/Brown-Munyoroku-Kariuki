    Brown Munyoroku Kariuki

    National Chairman - The Service Party

    Brown Munyoroku Kariuki is the National Chairman of The Service Party and one of its founding members. It is his desire to elevate, nurture, and empower the Kenyan youths into the country’s political scene that saw him participate in the formation of the party. Roysambu is the neighborhood where Munyoroku a.k.a Brown grew up, it is his home; it is here he learned about God and neighborhood and the very place where he wants to make a difference. As an entrepreneur, Brown has a wealth of experience in leadership with several investments in events, (Tents, chairs n decor business), General supplies, and Transport. His leadership qualities were apparent early on in his church, school, and college years, where he was elected to various leadership roles. He has been a junior youth church leader and a discipleship teacher. From a matatu tout in route 44 and is now the Chairman of route 44 Titikaka.
